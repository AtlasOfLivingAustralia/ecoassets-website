EcoAssets received investment (doi.org/10.47486/XN005) from the Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC). The ARDC is funded by the Australian Research Data National Collaborative Research Infrastructure Strategy (NCRIS).

The project partners acknowledge Australia’s Traditional Owners and pay respect to the past and present Elders of the nation’s Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities. We honour and celebrate the spiritual, cultural and customary connections of Traditional Owners to the country and the biodiversity that forms part of that country.
